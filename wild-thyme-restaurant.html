Wild Thyme Restaurant

Wild Thyme was created to celebrate modern Lebanese hospitality through bold flavors, warm service, and carefully sourced ingredients. The restaurant story starts with family, tradition, and a deep respect for the shared table.

Our Story

From the first menu draft to the first guest served, Wild Thyme has focused on food that feels both rooted and alive. Signature dishes are inspired by Levantine classics, refined with a contemporary point of view and a consistent standard of quality.

Every plate is designed to create a memorable experience: generous, vibrant, and welcoming. Wild Thyme continues to evolve while protecting the values that shaped it from day one.
