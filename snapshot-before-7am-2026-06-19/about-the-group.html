About the Group

Building hospitality brands that feel intimate, human, and unforgettable.

The Wild Thyme Group was founded to unify two ideas that rarely grow under one roof: operational excellence and emotional storytelling. As a holding company, we support each brand with strategic structure while preserving its soul, voice, and cultural identity.

Our Narrative

What started as one restaurant concept evolved into a multi-brand hospitality platform. We expanded intentionally, choosing projects that could deliver consistency at scale without losing warmth at the table.

Today, the group develops and operates brands across dining and catering, investing in systems, leadership, and long-term brand equity.

Vision

To create a portfolio of hospitality companies recognized for quality, trust, and cultural relevance.

Mission

To give each brand the strategic backbone it needs while preserving the character guests fall in love with.

Commitment

To measure success by long-term impact: guest loyalty, team growth, and the strength of every community we serve.
